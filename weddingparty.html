Andrew & Annie

We had our first date hiking at Monrovia Canyon Park. After a few more hikes, beach trips, movies, and lots of eating, we knew our lives were more fun with each other.

We are so excited to celebrate with all of you, as we move into this next chapter of our lives.

We’ve been to countless concerts together and have had many other adventures together too. In 2018, we took an impromptu trip to Spain with our mutual friend, Celina! Dayna is a talented, beautiful, and determined woman. She is also an incredibly talented artist! I am very lucky to call her my friend and maid of honor.
