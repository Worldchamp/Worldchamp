Andrew & Annie

We had our first date hiking at Monrovia Canyon Park in 2014. After a few more hikes, beach trips, movies, and delicious meals. we knew our lives were more fun with each other in them. Andrew proposed to Annie in December 2018 in a special location on the same hike, where it all began. We are both excited to have found a partner with whom we can take on all of life's adventures.

We are so excited to celebrate with all of you, as we move into this next chapter of our lives.
